How to play

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game
  2. There are Shadeglass Shards below. By clicking on a shard you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  3. You win the game by matching your score to the number on the left. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number
  4. The Value of each Crystal is hidden from you until you click on it
  5. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal
